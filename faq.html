FAQ - CarRentals

Q: How can I rent a car from CarRentals?
A: Renting a car with CarRentals is easy. You can make a reservation online through our website or by calling our customer support team. Simply select your desired rental dates, location, and car type, and follow the instructions to complete the booking process.
Q: What are the requirements for renting a car?
A: To rent a car from CarRentals, you must be at least 21 years old, have a valid driver's license, and a major credit card in your name. International renters may need additional documentation, so please check our Terms and Conditions for more details.
Q: Can I modify or cancel my reservation?
A: Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation. Log in to your account on our website or contact our customer support team for assistance. Please note that cancellation policies may vary based on the rental location and type of booking.
Q: What happens if I return the car late?
A: Late returns may incur additional charges. If you know you will be returning the car later than the agreed-upon time, please contact our rental office in advance to make arrangements. Late returns without prior notice may result in a late fee.
Q: Do you offer additional services like GPS or child seats?
A: Yes, we offer a range of additional services and accessories, including GPS navigation systems, child seats, and more. You can add these services during the booking process or request them at the rental counter.
Q: What should I do in case of an accident or breakdown?
A: In the event of an accident or breakdown, please contact our emergency assistance number provided in your rental agreement. We will guide you through the necessary steps to ensure your safety and resolve the situation as quickly as possible.
Q: Are there any mileage restrictions?
A: Many of our rental packages come with unlimited mileage. However, some special offers or long-term rentals may have mileage restrictions. Please check your rental agreement or contact our team for specific details about your reservation.
Q: How can I extend my rental period?
A: If you wish to extend your rental period, contact our rental office or customer support team as soon as possible. Extensions are subject to availability, and additional charges may apply for the extra days.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Debit cards and cash payments may be accepted with certain restrictions. Please review our payment policies for more information.
Q: Can I rent a car for someone else?
A: Yes, you can rent a car for someone else. However, the person driving the rental car must meet all the necessary requirements, including age, driver's license, and payment method.
